Motivation

As a non-major coming into this class, I was a bit unsure how well I could read Shakespeare. It did not take long for me to realize that comprehending the text was going to be a challenge. This was surprising to me given some of the materials that we reviewed at the start of the semester, including the "You're quoting Shakespeare" video, as seen below.

From the sounds of it, modern day English is heavily influenced by Shakespeare's works. What makes it seem so different, then, beyond iambic pentameter? This question motivated me to explore Shakespeare's English further and how it compares to modern texts.

In addition to contemplating these differences, I wanted to apply Shakespeare to my major. Machine learning is a booming field in Computer Science and is being used more and more everyday. Google and Apple use machine learning, for example, to determine what animals or things are in your photos, allowing you to search your personal photos for pictures of your dog. I wanted to see how these concepts could be applied to an English class.
